About Us

Machine Learning Intelligence is a financial technology startup offering computational finance engineering as applied to buy side retail and institutional segments. Quantitative strategies, risk models, research tools with advanced functionalities, that allow traders and investors to participate and invest across all markets and make better decisions in following changes across asset classes, market shock and risk. Empowering private investors to diversify from Private equity, Venture Capital, Real Estate and other industries to follow markets accurately without the fear of taking part of a non traditional industry. Supporting the capital markets toward better performance through regulation and transparency. Emphasizing the liquidity available in the capital markets over other traditional industries. Our goal is to open doors to accredited and non accredited investors by creating collaboration between retail segments, institutions, quantitative developers, strategy managers, and academics. Connecting data, analytics, implementation and application with a focus on performance, risk management and account control. Quantitative strategies that can be delivered in a variety of ways, often with lower capital requirements. With a focus on regulation and compliance, we aim to explore all possibilities on the table and offer investors access to better science. We aim to offer proprietary data, analytics, and feeds, standard market data, visualization and most importantly, direct access to state of the art quantitative strategy development built by the top engineers, statisticians, professors and managers. Beyond education to interpretation, beyond interpretation to application, beyond application to performance.
